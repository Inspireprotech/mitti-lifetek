System

SteriPOC™ system: point-of-care sterilisation at any healthcare setting

Step 01

SteriPOC™ is a compact, portable, AI-driven tabletop sterilisation and disinfection system designed for speed, safety, and sustainability. Paired with our proprietary, eco-friendly SteriPOC™ disinfection liquid, it delivers hospital-grade sterilisation in clinics, dental practices, mobile health units, and emergency care settings.

Step 02

The formulation not only prevents cross-infections but also disrupts and inhibits biofilm formation, ensuring a higher level of infection control. It is fully compatible with a wide range of surgical and dental instruments, preserving equipment integrity while maintaining proven antimicrobial efficacy — making infection prevention accessible anywhere.

Step 03

SteriPOC™ reduces cross-infection risks, protects patients and healthcare staff, and enhances operational efficiency in any care setting. Fully inspired with WHO infection prevention and control guidelines, it enables rapid sterilisation and disinfection of surgical and dental instruments, helping healthcare providers lower hospital-acquired infection rates, extend the lifespan of critical equipment, and reduce long-term operational costs.

OralAura™

OralAura™ : Specialised mouthwashes for patients who need more than ordinary care

OralAura™ is a portfolio of clinically formulated, alcohol-free mouthwashes developed for patients whose medical conditions make oral health a vital part of their treatment and recovery. Over 2 billion high-risk individuals worldwide, including those fighting cancer, diabetes, tuberculosis and HIV/AIDS, and cannot safely use conventional mouthwashes due to harmful chemicals and a lack of clinical validation. OralAura™ is designed to close this gap with safe, effective, and evidence-based solutions.

Our proprietary OralAuraTM formulations combine anti-inflammatory agents, specialised enzymes for wound-healing and restricting oral biofilms, and soothing components to deliver comprehensive oral care. This targeted approach offers proven antimicrobial action, reduces inflammation, promotes healing, and helps prevent secondary infections that could delay or complicate treatment.

We exist to tackle the world’s most overlooked health threats—missed diagnoses, untreated infections, unsafe medical equipment, and healthcare access gaps. Our mission is to transform these hidden challenges into breakthrough solutions that save lives and strengthen global health systems.

"One Health" is the idea that human, animal, and environmental health are deeply interconnected. By addressing health threats holistically, we can prevent outbreaks, improve diagnostics, and create sustainable medical solutions. Mitti Lifetek Medica is committed to advancing this approach through innovation.

We target silent but deadly issues that often go unnoticed, such as:

  • Missed or delayed diagnoses (e.g., TB, sepsis, antimicrobial resistance)
  • Untreated infections due to lack of access or awareness
  • Unsafe medical practices (contaminated equipment, improper sterilization)
  • Healthcare disparities in underserved communities

We combine cutting-edge technology, AI-driven diagnostics, and affordable medical innovations to bridge gaps in healthcare. Our solutions range from smart diagnostic tools to sterilization advancements, ensuring safer, more accessible care for all.

  • Patients in low-resource settings who face delayed or incorrect diagnoses
  • Healthcare workers who need reliable, low-cost tools
  • Communities vulnerable to outbreaks due to weak health infrastructure
  • Governments & NGOs working on preventive and responsive healthcare
